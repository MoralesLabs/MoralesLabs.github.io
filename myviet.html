MyViet

Practice Vietnamese, a little every day.

MyViet is a simple, focused Android app for building your Vietnamese vocabulary through active practice. Add your own words, organize them into categories, and drill them in flashcard-style sessions with instant feedback - also enjoy the new listening feature!

Get the app Back to all apps

Features

What's inside

Build your own word lists

Add English/Vietnamese pairs with optional descriptions — no fixed curriculum to follow.

Import from Excel

Bulk-add words by uploading a CSV exported from Excel (or any spreadsheet), with columns for English, Vietnamese, and optional description and categories.

Four practice modes

English→Vietnamese, Vietnamese→English, a random mix, and listen-only (hear the word, type what you understood).

Text-to-speech in both languages

Native audio reads English and Vietnamese aloud, with adjustable speech speed.

Read-all playback

One tap plays through your entire visible list for hands-free, passive review.

Categories and search

Organize words into custom categories and filter or search to find anything fast.

Progress tracking

Every word logs your correct and missed attempts so you can see what still needs work.

Personalization

Themes, zoom, and column controls to set up the app the way you like.

Works offline

Everything runs on your phone — no account or connection required.

Dataset Library

Ready-made word packs

MyViet includes a Library (premium) that lets you add curated vocabulary packs in one tap — no manual file picking. The app reads a catalog hosted here on this site, so new packs can appear without any app update.

To connect it, paste this catalog URL into the app under Settings → Library catalog (URL), then open Vocabulary → Library:

Catalog https://moraleslabs.github.io/library/datasets.json
