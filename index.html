About

Hi, I'm Sebastian.

SM

I'm a mechanical engineer with a background in the automotive manufacturing industry, where I focused on business-case development and automation solutions. A hands-on mindset and a real curiosity for learning led me to start building my own projects — and Morales Labs is the channel where I share them.

Those projects range widely: from complex machine learning models to small, focused apps — like one for practicing new languages. Whatever the scale, my work stays pragmatic, minimalist, and user-centric.

If you have an idea worth building and you're looking for a partner to help bring it to life — on the business or the operations side — don't hesitate to reach out. I'm always happy to talk through a good problem.
